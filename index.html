About

About

I empower startups and NGOs with pixel-perfect UI and human-centered UX.

I’m Chidiebere Johnbosco Nwolise, a UI/UX designer and BSc Computer Science graduate from Landmark University who blends technical know-how with creative flair. With strong HTML/CSS chops, growing JavaScript skills, and a background in data analysis and IT administration, I partner with startups and mission-driven nonprofits to craft intuitive, inclusive interfaces that solve real problems and spark joy. Guided by empathy and playful experimentation, I dive deep into user needs and iterate boldly to deliver impactful digital experiences. Every project is fueled by my relentless curiosity and commitment to driving real-world results, whether that’s boosting engagement, streamlining workflows, or championing social change.

I love clean, simple & unique design.

I deliver end-to-end digital solutions—from crafting intuitive UI/UX and responsive front-ends to providing reliable IT support, actionable data analysis, and strategic product management
